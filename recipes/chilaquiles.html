Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles are a traditional Mexican breakfast dish made with tortillas.

Ingredients

  • corn tortillas
  • corn oil
  • red chile sauce
  • springs of epazote
  • cotija cheese
  • mexican crema
  • cilantro
  • onion

Steps

  1. Fry the tortillas
  2. Heat the salsa
  3. Add fried tortillas to salsa with epazote
  4. Add crema, chopped cilantro and onion
